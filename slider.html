TV & Digital media producer

After I left theatre school, where I studied Drama Directing, I started to collaborate with TV broadcasters and major media companies in Poland. I started as a news editor at TVN24 where I was responsible for the Sunday edition of a weekly TV programme. I directed and produced various TV shows at TVN Style, where, among others, I was also responsible for the international documentary programming. I was a co-creator of “People of freedom”, a joint project of TVN & Agora celebrating 25 years of democratic Poland. For nc+ I’ve created “Be Active. Ewa Chodakowska”, a cookery & health celebrity show starring one of the most renowned Polish fitness coaches. As a Digital Media Producer in Teatr Wielki - Polish National Opera I was responsible for the creation of promotional and educational online content. Together with Human Ark, the biggest animation studio in Poland, I’ve created a successful animated mini-series for kids. You can watch it here. I try to follow the ever-changing digital market and new inspiring solutions for storytellers. I participate in transmedia labs, interactive workshops and occasionally, I work as an expert on media. Workshops & talks (logos & links): MEDIA Desk Poland Transmedia Lab NInA Wersja BETA / Digital Stories

Documentary filmmaker

I grew up in theatre, so stage always seemed to me like a natural place to be. At the age of 20, I’ve produced “Encounters”, the biggest international theatre festival in Warsaw, where I’ve met some amazing, world-renowned artists: The Wooster Group, Robert Wilson, Heiner Goebbels, Joseph Nadj, Krystian Lupa and many more. Already a theatre student, I’ve started to work with found footage and video edition. I’ve created a multimedia dance show “We Are Oh So Lucky” and a video based one-man-show “Street Fighting Years”. I also made several video projects for Polish and European art centers: Centre for Contemporary Art Ujazdowski Castle, TR Warszawa, Nowy Teatr, National Centre for Culture, Bec Zmiana New Culture Foundation. During the Festival d’Avignon, I’ve met a Belgian artist Jan Lauwers and Needcompany with whom I commenced to work on my first documentary film project. In 2012, PLANETE+ Doc Film Festival screened my debut, an awarded Polish-Belgian documentary “I Want (No) Reality. Needcompany on Life and Art”, which, later on, was broadcasted by CANAL+ and TVP (Polish National Television). Since then, I have been an active member of the international film industry; in 2013 I was a Juror at the 13th T-Mobile New Horizons IFF. I also started to explore the emerging interactive & transmedia sector (you can find some of my texts about it here). Currently, I’m developping “The Future of Forever”, an American non-fiction feature about life extension and the future of mankind. I simultaneously work on new documentary projects with independent film and TV producers. Workshops & talks (logos & links):

Writer and translator

I write media and I write about media. After having worked for almost ten years as a film translator for major companies like SDI MEDIA, I wrote dozens of scripts for TV and Internet broadcasters. I’ve been writing articles, reportages and op-eds for: Wysokie Obcasy (High Heels, weekly magazine of “Gazeta Wyborcza”) wysokieobcasy.pl Gazeta Wyborcza (major Polish daily) / sport.pl gazeta.pl Polish Art & Culture magazines - Dwutygodnik, Didaskalia, Kultura Liberalna And I’ve been a successful book translator: Bernard-Marie Koltès "Une part de ma vie. Entretiens 1983–1989" "No Beauty for Me There Where Human Life is Rare. On Jan Lauwers’ Theatre Work with Needcompany"
